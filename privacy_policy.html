Privacy Policy

App: Parking App
Last updated: May 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Parking App ("the App") collects, uses, and handles user and device data. We are committed to transparency and to handling data responsibly.

1. Data We Collect

The App may process the following categories of data:

  • Location data (precise/approximate): Used to show your current position on the map and support nearby parking search/navigation features.
  • Device and app identifiers: Collected by advertising services (such as Google AdMob) to deliver and measure ads.
  • Network/request data: Basic technical data (for example IP address, request metadata) may be processed by the services used to fetch parking information and map tiles.

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide core app functions, including map display and parking information.
  • Show your current location when you grant location permission.
  • Load and refresh parking data from public/open data sources.
  • Display ads through AdMob to support app operation and maintenance.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared with third-party service providers only as needed to provide app functionality:

  • Google AdMob: Ad serving and ad performance measurement.
  • Map and data providers: OpenStreetMap tile services and public parking-data endpoints required for map and parking content.

4. Permissions

The App may request the following Android permissions:

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION: To provide location-based features.
  • INTERNET / ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: To download map and parking data and display ads.

You can deny location permission, but some location-based features may be unavailable.

5. Data Retention

The App primarily uses data in real time to provide map and parking features. Some app data may be cached on your device to improve performance. We do not maintain a user account database for this App.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 unless explicitly stated in the store listing. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

If your target audience includes children under 13, you must ensure your app configuration, ad settings, and data practices comply with applicable laws and Google Play policies, including the Families Policy and User Data policy.

7. Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect data in transit and on device. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Third-Party Services

The App may rely on third-party services. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob
  • OpenStreetMap

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with the revised "Last updated" date.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us using the developer contact information shown on the app's Google Play listing.
